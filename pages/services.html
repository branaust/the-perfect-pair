DAY OF
Made for couples who have completed all wedding preparations and booked all their selected vendors but needs a professional wedding coordinator to execute and manage their wedding day! We will be alongside the two of you as we plan, arrange, and design the broad and intricate details of your ideal wedding day. You've done all of the hard work, spent many late nights making those decisions about every little detail of your wedding day but you’re probably not imagining yourself checking the table plan and place settings part-way through your make-up application? With my On The Day Coordination package I will create your wedding day plan, coordinate your suppliers and be there on the day to manage the entire wedding, from set up to finish. I will ensure that everything comes together exactly as you imagined it, giving you, and your families the freedom to enjoy the lead up to the day and the big day itself, with the peace of mind that everything is being taken care of by professionals.
